About us

We are Rodrigo and Sarah, two UX designers diving into the evolving world of digital design.

Our paths crossed in 2023 while studying at Hyper Island in Karlskrona, Sweden - and together we created UXESS, born from our commitment to accessibility, and to make digital experiences better for everyone.

Our blog is where we connect the dots between innovation, usability, and the real people behind the screens. We explore the latest in UX/UI patterns, digital trends, and tools that are shaping the future.

Whether you're a fellow designer, or someone who just loves digital stuff, we're glad you're here.
