Hey there!

We're Rodrigo and Sarah, and this is UXESS.

Rodrigo Figueiredo

Rodrigo is a UX/UI Designer and Accessibility Specialist with a blend of creative vision and practical expertise.

Before transitioning into tech, Rodrigo spent nearly a decade in leadership roles, where he led international visual merchandising and store design teams across Sweden, the UK, and Finland. That chapter of his career sharpened his skills in communication, team management, and storytelling - all of which now feed into his UX practice.

Currently creating accessible, responsive, and beautifully structured digital products. His technical skills include Figma, accessible design systems, design tokens, prototyping, ARIA patterns, and detailed design annotations for seamless developer handoff.

He works closely with WCAG 2.2 guidelines, and auditing websites to ensure inclusive experiences for everyone.

Sarah Beu

Sarah is a UX designer and Accessibility Specialist with a strong background in digital solutions, regulatory compliance, and user-centered design.

With over a decade of experience in project management and tax technology, she led 20+ IT implementations across industries - ensuring clients stayed compliant, and ahead of the curve.

Today, Sarah brings her deep analytical skills into the world of accessible design. She's expanding her expertise in WCAG 2.2 guidelines, semantic design tokens, and inclusive design systems. She conducts accessibility audits, refines handoff processes, and explores how technology can remove barriers for all users.

She's also the co-founder and design lead of Arcanimal, a social startup focused on improving animal adoption in Brazil.
